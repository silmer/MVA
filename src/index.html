Meirverdiavgift

Without comfortably landing in one genre, Meirverdiavgift plays music ranging somewhere from pop-rock to ... prog, maybe? Perhaps Vegard can come up with a more precise and lengthy description. Anyways, you can clearly see we're leaning towards prog from looking at this awesome photo to the right.

The band consists of Morgaine Wood (Mog), Vegard Lillevoll, Andreas Lind-Johansen and Silje Dahl.
