Meirverdiavgift

Since December 2015, Meirverdiavgift has been playing their peculiar mix of electric and acoustic rock coupled with the occasional folk number. Their lyrical style range from apocalyptic drama to video game parodies.

They currently consist of Morgaine Wood on vocals and bass, Vegard Lillevoll on the electric violin, backup vocals and piano, Andreas Lind-Johansen on guitar and backup vocals and Silje Dahl on drums.

Spring 2017, we're working on improving our recordings. We're even planning on playing at a small festival in July, so stay tuned for updates!
